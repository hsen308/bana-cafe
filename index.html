Our Story

A Place to Gather
in Hamra

Nestled in the vibrant streets of Hamra, Beirut, Bana was born from a simple belief — that great coffee, honest food, and warm company can transform any space into something extraordinary.

From specialty single-origin espresso to our beloved Shakshuka, Bana Signature sandwiches and artisan desserts — every item is crafted with intention, using the finest local ingredients and the warmth of Lebanese hospitality.
